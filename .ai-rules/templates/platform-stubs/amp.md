# AI Development Rules

Follow the AI development rules defined in `.ai-rules/AGENTS.md` and all
referenced rule files in `.ai-rules/rules/` before beginning any feature work.

Triggerable workflow skills live in `.ai-rules/skills/`. Amp does not
auto-discover skill folders; invoke a skill by reading its `SKILL.md` when
the trigger condition described in its frontmatter applies.
